About Rayna

I'm a front end web developer based out of Boulder, Colorado. I've been building Wordpress sites for almost eight years and am currently studying to be a full stack web developer in the University of Denver's Coding Bootcamp.

Current skills include: Wordpress, Bootstrap, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, Firebase and HTML.

Along side my technical background, I also bring eight years of experience as a professional photographer, four years of experience as a freelance SEO specialist and almost six years as a dedicated dog mom.
